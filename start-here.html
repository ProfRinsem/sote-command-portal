Your first five minutes in SOTE

A new student should be able to follow this sequence during orientation, an open house, or the first lab meeting.

1. Enter the portal

Start at the digital front door and learn that SOTE is a student-operated technology ecosystem, not just a lab full of equipment.

2. Read the map

Open the Environment Map and see how Mission Control, Rocket’s Forge, Ace’s Support Command, Patch Bay Alpha, Rocket’s Launch Pad, and the Cyber Defense Command fit together.

3. Meet the guides

Each character has a role: Ace supports students and help desk readiness, Professor Rocket teaches engineering discipline, Sir Kingston guards the cyber range, SOTEE explains the system, and Cypher Raven creates controlled challenge.

4. Choose a first mission

Open the Mission Board and pick a beginner mission such as Cable Runner, Switch Initiate, or Help Desk Hero.

5. Save evidence

Every completed mission should produce an artifact: a worksheet, screenshot, ticket note, build log, diagram, reflection, or approved portfolio entry.

Start Here page purpose
