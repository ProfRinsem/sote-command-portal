Ask SOTEE

The future controlled SOTE knowledge assistant.

Ask SOTEE is the planned kiosk and knowledge assistant that can answer questions using approved documentation, tickets, build logs, NetBox/IPAM records, command references, and troubleshooting guides.

Example Questions

What students could ask

  • What is Rocket's Forge?
  • What mission should I try first?
  • What is a VLAN?
  • Show me today's open student tasks.
  • Explain what this rack does.
  • What certification connects to this lab?
Guardrails

Controlled knowledge layer

SOTEE should answer from approved SOTE documentation, not guess. It should avoid passwords, production credentials, sensitive IP details, and anything not approved for public or student use.

Kiosk Path

Mission Control presence

The future kiosk can be a tablet, touchscreen, or small workstation paired with the SOTEE owl identity, voice output, mission briefs, and guided student onboarding.
