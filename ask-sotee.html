Ask SOTEE

The future SOTE AI operations kiosk and knowledge assistant.

This page keeps Ask SOTEE visible as a portal capability instead of losing it during the visitor tour rebuild.

Ask

Students and visitors ask a controlled SOTE question.

Retrieve

SOTEE looks at approved docs, status boards, tickets, and mission content.

Explain

SOTEE answers in student-friendly operational language.

Act

The user gets a safe next step, checklist, or ticket suggestion.

Safety boundary

Ask SOTEE should only use approved SOTE documentation, public visitor language, sanitized status content, and instructor-approved troubleshooting guides. It should not expose credentials, internal-only security details, student data, or production CCAC information.
