Chief Engineer and Forge Master

Professor Rocket is the mythic builder of SOTE. He treats every cable, switch, VM, rack, and build log as part of the Forge.

Home base: Rocket’s Forge, Rocket’s Engineering Bay, Rocket’s Launch Pad
