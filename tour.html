Start Tour

Welcome to SOTE

Students do not just study technology. They operate it.

Student sees: A branded command portal that makes the ecosystem feel real.

Mission focus: Access. Learn. Build. Defend. Lead.

Stop 1

CEIT 325 Classroom

The classroom is the student launch point for operating the ecosystem.

Student sees: Workstations, instruction, and access terminals.

Mission focus: Begin with First Signal and Technology Cadet readiness.

Stop 2

Mission Control

The operator floor for network status, troubleshooting, console access, and briefings.

Student sees: Switching, console workflows, status boards, and command-center language.

Mission focus: Observe, connect, diagnose, and resolve.

Stop 3

Rocket's Forge

The teaching datacenter where students build, provision, host, and orchestrate.

Student sees: Servers, Proxmox, storage, templates, and infrastructure services.

Mission focus: Rocket Approved builds and repeatable documentation.

Stop 4

Western Pennsylvania Cyber Defense Command

The cyber range where students practice defense, investigation, and recovery.

Student sees: Cyber range systems, scenarios, and Sir Kingston watchlist identity.

Mission focus: Detect, defend, investigate, and recover.

Stop 5

Patch Bay Alpha

The physical-layer and security-edge staging bay.

Student sees: Orange wall, blue cabling, patch rack, cable tray, and security-edge staging.

Mission focus: CONNECT. PATCH. VERIFY.

Stop 6

Rocket's Engineering Bay

The R&D and hardware staging space for server prep, parts validation, and experiments.

Student sees: Staged hardware, rails, parts, and engineering workbench workflows.

Mission focus: Inspect, stage, test, and document.

Final

Mission Complete

Visitors can complete First Signal and claim the Technology Cadet badge.

Student sees: A pathway from visitor to Student Technology Corps member.

Mission focus: Turn curiosity into participation.
