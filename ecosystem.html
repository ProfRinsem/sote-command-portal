How a student moves through SOTE

Technology Cadet

Learns the language of the ecosystem, explores spaces, completes orientation, and practices documentation.

Help Desk Technician

Works through support tickets, identity tasks, user communication, and service desk documentation.

Infrastructure Technician

Validates cabling, switches, VLANs, Proxmox hosts, VMs, and controlled infrastructure changes.

Mission Control Operator

Monitors status, follows command references, documents incidents, and supports live lab operations.

Cybersecurity Analyst

Investigates approved cyber range events, documents evidence, and supports defensive recovery scenarios.
