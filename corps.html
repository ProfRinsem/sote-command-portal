Student Technology Corps

A visible path from first signal to employment-ready professional.

Students progress through increasingly complex operational roles while earning badges, certifications, experience, and confidence.

01

Technology Cadet

First Signal orientation, safety, documentation, and lab citizenship.

02

Help Desk Technician

Ticket intake, support, identity tasks, and ACE Service Desk workflows.

03

Infrastructure Technician

Switching, cabling, Proxmox, servers, templates, and validation tasks.

04

Mission Control Operator

Status boards, console access, monitoring, incident notes, and escalation.

05

Cybersecurity Analyst

Cyber range missions, detection, defense, investigation, and recovery.

06

Certified Professional

Certification preparation, validation, internship readiness, and employment.

Badge Alignment

Progress becomes visible.

The badge system gives students and visitors a clear way to see what has been learned and what comes next.
