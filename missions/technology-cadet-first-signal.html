Technology Cadet: First Signal

This is the first SOTE mission. It is designed for new students, visitors, high school groups, elementary groups, open houses, and first-day orientation.

Start the Mission View Badge Print Badge Sheet

Your mission briefing

SOTEE has detected a new signal entering the ecosystem. Your job is to learn what SOTE is, identify the major zones, meet the characters, and choose the pathway you want to explore next.

Mission goal

Prove you can navigate SOTE safely.

Complete the short guided activity and show that you understand the basics of the ecosystem without touching private systems, credentials, or sensitive infrastructure.

Badge earned

Technology Cadet

Visitors can receive a physical badge, sticker, or card. Students can use this as the first entry in their Student Technology Corps journey.

Mission checklist

Check off each item as you complete it. This demo runs in the browser only and does not save private information.

0 of 5 steps complete.

First Signal received.

You have completed the Technology Cadet demo mission. A student guide, instructor, or tour leader can now issue the physical Technology Cadet badge, sticker, or card if this is being used during an approved visit.

What to do during the mission

This version is designed to be usable immediately during a tour.

Step 1

Say what SOTE is

Example: “SOTE is a student-operated technology ecosystem where students build, support, document, and defend real learning infrastructure.”

Step 2

Find three zones

Pick any three: Mission Control, Rocket’s Forge, Ace’s Support Command, Patch Bay Alpha, Rocket’s Launch Pad, or Cyber Defense Command.

Step 3

Meet the characters

Choose two characters and explain what they represent. SOTEE guides, Ace supports, Rocket builds, Kingston defends, and Cypher Raven challenges.

Tour guide script

Student guide says

“Congratulations. You just completed your first SOTE mission. You learned the story, found the zones, met the characters, and chose a pathway. That makes you a Technology Cadet.”

Evidence

Approved evidence

  • Stamped visitor card
  • Short reflection
  • Tour checklist
  • Photo of physical badge, if approved
  • No passwords, private student data, internal IPs, or sensitive screenshots
