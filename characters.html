The story layer

Visitors enter SOTE through a digital front door. SOTEE guides them. Ace shows the student support mission. Professor Rocket opens the Forge. Sir Kingston stands watch over the cyber range. Cypher Raven creates controlled challenges that students learn to defeat.

Canon story hook: SOTE is a training ground where students become technology heroes by learning to support users, build infrastructure, write tools, defend systems, and bring clarity to digital chaos.
