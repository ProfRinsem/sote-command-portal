The streamlined process

The goal is simple: mission report in, portal update pack out.

1. Work happens

Students and faculty complete a build day, tour, validation, documentation sprint, or mission activity.

2. Mission report submitted

The report captures what changed, what was completed, blockers, next actions, student wins, and safe notes.

3. Public-safe update drafted

Private notes are separated from public notes. No credentials, student private data, internal secrets, or sensitive configurations are published.

4. Portal files updated

The Projects board, individual project pages, What Changed This Week, blockers, missions, badges, and changelog are updated.

5. Review and publish

The new static package is reviewed locally, approved, and uploaded to Apache.
