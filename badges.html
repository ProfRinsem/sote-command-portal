Active badge

Technology Cadet: First Signal

Complete the entry mission: identify three SOTE zones, meet two characters, explain one student pathway, and choose one mission you would try next. Visitors can leave with a physical sticker, card, patch, or printed badge.

  • Audience: visitors, high school groups, elementary groups, first-semester students
  • Evidence: quick tour card, stamp sheet, or short reflection
  • Safety: no private systems, no credentials, no sensitive details

Badge rule

A badge should mean “I completed approved work and can show appropriate evidence,” not “I watched someone else do it.” Physical visitor badges are welcome, but student portfolio badges should still connect to clear evidence.
