Zone 6 - R&D, staging, and hardware preparation

Rocket's Engineering Bay

Rocket's Engineering Bay is the workbench environment for hardware staging, server preparation, parts validation, and engineering experiments.

Student Sees

What this zone makes visible

Bench space, spare servers, rails, storage components, parts staging, hardware inspection, and student engineering notes.

Mission Focus

Students learn that professional infrastructure work begins before equipment is racked: inspect, stage, label, test, and document.

Student tasks

  • Inspect staged equipment
  • Record serial/model information
  • Prepare rails or cabling
  • Move parts into the correct locker or ticket

System notes

  • Supports R730/R820/R930 staging
  • Connects to Rocket's Parts Locker and Quartermaster Bay concept
  • Good zone for hardware inventory and pre-build tickets
